Sokoban is a puzzle game where the player moves boxes 
through a maze to designated storage spots. 
The challenge is that boxes can only be pushed, not pulled, 
and the player can only move one box at a time. 
The game requires careful planning and strategy to solve the puzzles.

Explanations of board :
# - borders of the board
Y - designated storage spots
X - boxes
@ - your character
W - box at the storage spot
Z - character at the storage spot

Explanations of movement :
W - move UP
S - move DOWN
A - move left
D - move right

First of all, at the start of a game, you need to type "yes" to
start and load the game. Then you need to choose level from range
1-10. After this you need to just move your character with [W/A/S/D]
